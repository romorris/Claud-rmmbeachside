# Dog Beach

One of the nearest beaches to the condo is the southern end of Jupiter Dog Beach—an incredible spot where pets can enjoy the sand and surf right alongside their owners. If you’re staying at the condo, this beach offers endless fun for both you and your four-legged companion.

The dog-friendly section spans 2.5 miles, from beach marker #26 at Marcinski Road north to beach marker #57 at the Carlin Park property line. With plenty of open shoreline to explore, your pup can safely run, play, and experience the joy of being off-leash.

You’ll also find convenient water stations to keep pets hydrated, as well as waste disposal stations placed throughout the area to help maintain a clean and welcoming beach environment. Please remember to pick up after your pet to keep this beautiful stretch of coast enjoyable for everyone.

!!! tip "Jupiter Dog Beach is one of the few remaining dog-friendly beaches in the state of Florida."
   



The [Dog Beach](https://www.jupiter.fl.us/437/Dogs-on-the-Beach?nid=437) is a popular place, not just for dogs.
