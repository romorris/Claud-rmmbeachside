### Guest Access  
Designated parking is available, along with ample guest parking. A parking pass will be issued at the on-site office.  
  
***Important: TRUCKS ARE NOT PERMITTED*** to park on the premises according to association rules.  
  
Please complete the rental application and agreement prior to check-in, which will be emailed to you. A government-issued ID is required at least 4 days before receiving the parking permit.  
  
### Important Notes  
* Please contact Michelle regarding any pets, as the association has a pet policy with weight restrictions.  
* **Absolutely NO PARTIES** are allowed on the property or within the condo unit.  
* **NO SMOKING** is permitted in the condo.  
  
There is separate parking available for pickup trucks on the next street over, which is less than a 5-minute walk away. Further details can be found in the lease agreement.
